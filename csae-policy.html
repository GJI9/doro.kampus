Policy Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

DORO maintains a zero-tolerance policy against any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or child sexual exploitation (CSAE).

  • All CSAE-related content and behavior are strictly prohibited on our platform.
  • Any violation will result in immediate removal of content and termination of the account.
  • We cooperate with law enforcement authorities and will report CSAE-related activities as required by law.
  • We comply with international standards (UN CRC, ITU, ECPAT) and applicable Indonesian laws (UU ITE, UU PDP).

Report any CSAE-related content to: support@dorokampus.com
